# Source Policy

- **SoccerStats & Forebet**: External sources, may change format.
- Permitted public pages only.
- Read and respect robots.txt.
- Rate limits enforced (max 50 requests/run, 3s delay).
- No member content scraping.
- No bypassing of access controls.
- Cache pages and deduplicate requests.
- Live collection is disabled by default. Requires `--confirm-live`.
